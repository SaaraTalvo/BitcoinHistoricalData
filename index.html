Historical data for bitcoin


On this website you can find some specific historical data for bitcoin in a time traveller fashion.

Data can be found from 04/28/2013 until the present day. Date format month/day/year.

Input the starting and ending dates as advised above to find out...


...how many days is the longest bearish downward trend for bitcoin within the given date range?

...the date with the highest trading volume and the volume on that day in euros, within the given date range.

...a pair of days: The best day to buy and the best day to sell the bought bitcoin in the given date range for maximum profit.
